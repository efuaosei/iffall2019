Coffee Mug French Toast

A simple french toast recipe you can make in your dorm room microwave!

Prep Time: 5mins | Cook Time: 2mins | Yield: 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbls butter (sub: olive oil)
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbls milk (sub: almond milk or soy milk)
  • dash of cinnamon
  • a drop or 2 of vanilla extract (optional)

    Directions

  1. Dice it!

    Start with the bread. Dice, cut, and/or cube bread so that it's able to fit comfortable in the coffee mug.

  2. Butter it!

    Melt butter in cup for a few seconds in your microwave. Swoosh it around the cup to cover the entire inside.

    if substituting olive oil, skip warming the oil, and just swoosh it around your cup.

  3. Add it!

    Add your diced bread pieces to your cup

  4. Stir it!

    In a seperate cup, or bowl, mix the egg, the milk, dash of cinnamon, and the drop of vanilla extract. Stir!

  5. Mix it!

    Pour liquid over the bread. Smush the bread slightly to allow the mixture to soak all throughout the cup.

  6. Cook it!

    Microwave time! Place microwave-safe mug into the microwave and start with 1 minute, then set 10 secs at a time until it is cooked to yoyr liking (or until there i no more egg running).

  7. Enjoy it!

    Add syrup as desired, and enjoy your microwaved delicacy!
